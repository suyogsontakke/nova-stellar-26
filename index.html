About Nova Stellar

Nova Stellar 2026 is the flagship cultural fest of St. Francis De Sales College, Nagpur, designed to celebrate creativity, talent, and youthful energy. Set against a vibrant and dynamic backdrop, the event brings together students from across colleges to showcase their skills, passion, and artistic excellence.

Whether you’re an artist, performer, foodie, or fashion enthusiast — Nova Stellar has something for everyone. Step in, participate, and let your star shine bright!

Frequently Asked Questions

Is registration free for all events?
👉 The registration details sheet can be accessed from the “Registration” tab in the menu.

Is there any registration fee for the performing arts?
👉 Yes, registration is required in advance for auditions. Shortlisted participants must complete final registration and pay applicable fees after the audition round.

Can students from other colleges participate?
👉 Yes, this is an inter-collegiate event and students from all colleges are welcome to participate in the activities listed in the Inter-Collegiate panel.

Will participants receive certificates?
👉 Yes, all participants will receive certificates, except those eliminated in the audition rounds.
